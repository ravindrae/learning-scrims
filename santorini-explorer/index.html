Explore Santorini

Stunning caldera, dramatic landscapes, whitewashed buildings, iconic sunsets, and unique volcanic beaches

Top places to visit in Santorini

Red Beach

Unique red-hued sand and volcanic landscape, a popular tourist attraction located in the Akrotiri area, accessible by car, bus, or boat.

Santo Wines

A winery and wine tourism center in Pyrgos, with beautiful architecture, and offers tours, tastings, and sunset views of the caldera.

Caldera

A large, mostly submerged caldera, located in the southern Aegean Sea, 120 kilometers north of Crete in Greece.
